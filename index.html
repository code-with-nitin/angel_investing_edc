Meet your Speaker

Amit Gupta

From writing code in the early 2000s to writing investment cheques today, Amit’s journey through the Indian startup ecosystem has been nothing short of adventurous. In the past 15+ years, he’s played almost every role in the book — coder, engineering lead, product manager, product head, strategist, and now, full-time angel investor.

As an investor, Amit has built an impressive record — clocking a 105% XIRR over six years, backing some of the most promising startups of this decade. Through his Angel Syndicate, he connects disruptive founders with a community of 1000+ aspiring entrepreneurs and investors.

At his heart, Amit is drawn to startups that solve tough, real-world problems with immediate demand — from deeptech to defence tech. For him, angel investing is more than just investing money; it’s about fueling innovation, empowering founders, and helping shape the future.
